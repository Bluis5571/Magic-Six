About Magic Six

Six disciplines. One standard: precision.

Magic Six Technical Services FZC is a UAE free zone company delivering engineering and technical services to government authorities, developers, industrial facilities, hotels and commercial properties — with the rigor of a consultancy and the responsiveness of a dedicated maintenance partner.

Mission

To deliver technically excellent, safe and reliable services that protect and enhance the value of every facility we touch.

Vision

To be the UAE's most trusted technical services partner for government, industrial and commercial clients.

Core Values

Which areas of the UAE does Magic Six serve?

We operate across all seven emirates, serving government entities, developers, industrial facilities, hotels, commercial towers and residential communities.
